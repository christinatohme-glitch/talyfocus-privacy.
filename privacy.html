Talyfocous Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Summary

Talyfocous is a Windows desktop accounting application. Your company data is stored on your computer by default. We do not operate a central server that stores your books, inventory, or payroll.

Information the app stores on your device

  • Accounting data you enter: invoices, journal vouchers, chart of accounts, inventory, payroll, bank reconciliation files, and reports.
  • User accounts and settings you configure in the app.
  • Company files and fiscal-year data saved in a local database on your PC.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, or sell your accounting data to third parties.
  • We do not receive your payment card details.
  • We do not track your browsing activity inside or outside the app.
  • We do not build advertising profiles from your use of Talyfocous.

Optional features

  • Microsoft Store subscription — Microsoft processes payment and subscription status. Talyfocous checks whether your subscription is active using the Windows Store API. We do not receive your card number.
  • Company Network Link — if you connect computers on your office network, data is shared only within your local network as you configure it.
  • Cloud backup (optional) — if you sign in and use cloud backup, your backup is stored in your connected cloud storage account. This is optional.

How information is used

  • To run the app and save your work on your computer.
  • To let linked computers access data from the main computer, when you enable that feature.
  • To verify an active Microsoft Store subscription, when you install from the Store.
  • To upload or restore a backup, only when you choose cloud backup.

Data retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, uninstall the application, or remove company files.

Children's privacy

Talyfocous is business software and is not directed at children under 13.
